About Me

Based in Detroit, Julie enjoyed beginning her career working in artistic institutions, the Main Art Theatre and Pewabic Pottery. Both gave her the chance to explore her creative side while honing skills in management and collaborative work.

But after almost 10 years in customer service, she needed a change. After stumbling upon a website that sang the praises of a career in tech, she decided to try a little coding herself. Web development was the perfect next step for a gal that loves design and creating efficient user experiences. She found a love for coding which led her to an intensive Front-End Development Bootcamp through Grand Circus Detroit. Working in a small group to hone her skills, she now feels confident in starting her career as a dev.
